### Troubleshooting

**Camera not working?**
- Check browser permissions
- Allow camera access
- Try different camera source

**Face not detected?**
- Ensure good lighting
- Position face clearly
- Remove sunglasses/mask

**Login issues?**
- Clear browser cache
- Check credentials
- Try demo: officer/rto123
